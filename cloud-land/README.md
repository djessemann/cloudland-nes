# INSTRUCTION BOOKLET

---

### Thank you for selecting Cloud Land!

Please read this instruction booklet thoroughly before
beginning your adventure in the sky.

---

## THE STORY

High above the world, a brave little cat lives among
the clouds. Every night before bed, the cat must collect
glowing hearts scattered across the floating platforms
of Cloud Land. But beware — mischievous birds patrol
the skies, and one wrong move could send you tumbling!

Collect all the hearts. Reach the top. Say goodnite.

---

## GETTING STARTED

Press **START** at the title screen to begin your
adventure on Level 1.

---

## CONTROLS

```

| Control Pad (Left/Right) | Move your cat |
| A Button | Jump (hold for a higher jump!) |
| START | Pause / Unpause / Advance screens |

*The B button and SELECT are not used.*

---

## HOW TO PLAY

Your goal in each level is to collect **10 hearts**.
Hearts appear floating near the cloud platforms — grab
them before they vanish!

Once you've collected 10 hearts, the level is clear
and you advance to the next sky.

There are **5 levels** in Cloud Land. Complete them all
to win the game!

---

## SCORING

- Each heart collected is worth **1 point**
- Your score carries across all levels
- The maximum score is **50** (10 hearts x 5 levels)
- If you lose a life, your score reverts to what it was
  at the start of that level

---

## LIVES

You begin your journey with **5 lives**.

You lose a life if:
- A bird flies into you
- You fall off the bottom of the screen

When all lives are lost, it's **GAME OVER** and you
must start over from Level 1.

---

## FRIENDS AND FOES

### Your Cat
A scrappy purple feline with bright yellow eyes and
pink paws. Small but mighty — and surprisingly good
at jumping on things.

### Birds
Four birds patrol each level, flying back and forth
across the sky. They bob up and down as they go.
Watch their patterns and time your movements!

**Tip:** If you land on TOP of a bird, you'll stomp it
and bounce off safely! The bird will return after a few
seconds, flying a little faster than before.

### Hearts
Glowing hearts appear near the cloud platforms. They
don't stick around forever, so grab them quickly!
Up to 2 hearts can be on screen at once.

---

## HELPFUL HINTS

- **Hold the A button** for a higher jump. Tap it
  lightly for a short hop.

- **Stomp the birds!** Landing on a bird from above
  defeats it and gives you a safe bounce. Hold A during
  the bounce to go even higher.

- **Hearts are on a timer.** If you see one, go for it
  before it disappears.

- **Pause if you need a breather.** Press START during
  gameplay to pause. The music will wait for you.

- **Falling off is fatal.** Stay on the platforms!

---


*Now go collect those hearts. Goodnite!*
